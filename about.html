You should try film

Imagine buying yourself a present online after a night of heavy drinking. The next day, you vaguely remember what you ordered, but not quite. After a couple days, you get a package from yourself. It can be something truly amazing, a barely useful household item, or it can blow up glitter in your face. That's what film photography can feel like.

Escape instant gratification. Try analog.



This website

I launched Funky Side of Town in 2004 on Movable Type as a photoblog. Around 2008/2009, I took down digital photos, and in 2018 I switched to a custom, no-database Javascript setup hosted on Github.
